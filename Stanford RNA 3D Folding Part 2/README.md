## 📌 Overview

This competition challenges participants to predict the **3D structure of RNA molecules** — one of the most important and unsolved problems in modern biology. Accurate RNA structure prediction has massive implications for drug discovery, synthetic biology, and understanding diseases.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Predict the **3D atomic coordinates** of RNA sequences by modeling how a given RNA string folds into its three-dimensional shape.

---

## 🛠️ Approach

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on RNA sequence data
- Feature engineering based on nucleotide sequence patterns
- Applied deep learning / machine learning techniques for structure prediction
- Optimized model performance through cross-validation and hyperparameter tuning
- Generated final predictions for submission
